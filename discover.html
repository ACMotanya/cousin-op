If you are an existing customer and would like to make purchases online, please email customerservice@laurajanelle.com with your company name and email address to obtain a login.

To become a wholesale customer with Laura Janelle, please complete this application form. This is not a credit application. We require a $200.00 opening order. You may apply for credit terms through our application process. Please contact Customer Service for more information.

Required Documents

Documentation of an active online business, Etsy, Artfire or similar account, a trade show contract or a copy of an invoice where you have purchased from another wholesale company.

A copy of a Municipal occupational license from your local area, your Federal Tax ID or EIN, LLC, DBA or Vendor permit.

NOTE: If your business is in the State of Florida, a copy of a Tax ID must be provided also. STATE OF FLORIDA COMPTROLLER SALES TAX DIVISION RULE 39
