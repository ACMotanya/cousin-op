Frequently Asked Questions

Is ordering online secure?
Yes. We take the utmost care with the information that you provide us when placing an order on our website (or through any other means). The server that hosts our store encrypts the transmission of all credit card and personal customer information using the Internet-standard SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) protocol. During the encryption process, information is scrambled into small bits of code that cannot be read as they travel to us over the Internet. Once we receive the encrypted information, we use a private, key to decode it.

All of the information you provide during the ordering process is restricted to our staff, and we make sure that all of our employees up-to-date on our security and privacy policies. If you have further questions about the security of ordering online from Laura Janelle, please feel free to e-mail us at info@laurajanelle.com.
How do I place my order?
Once you have finished adding items to your shopping cart and are ready to complete your transaction, move your cursor to the upper-right corner of the screen and click on the “Cart” link. Then click the "Proceed to Checkout" button.

You will be prompted to enter the name and address of the intended recipient of your shipment (i.e. your delivery information). After providing this information, you will need to click on the "Place Order" link.

You will then notice a small window slide down and you will be transferred to our secure server and you can proceed checkout with a credit card.
How do I view what’s in my shopping cart?
To view the contents of your cart, click on the “View cart” icon in the upper-right corner of your computer screen, or click on the "Cart" link on the navigation bar. Once you are on the Cart page, you can easily change the quantity you want to purchase of a particular item in your cart by updating the quantity listed and then clicking the “Update cart” link. You can also delete any item in your cart by clicking the “Trash” icon to the left of that item.
How do I add items to my cart?
There are two ways to add an item. You can click the “Add to Cart” link on the shop page that displays all our products or, navigate to the detail view page of the item you are interested in and then choose your desired amount of that item. When you click the “Add to Cart” link, you will be brought to your "Shopping Cart" page where you can then change the quantity of the item that you would like to order using the "Quantity" field on the right side of the screen. After you have entered a quantity, you can continue shopping or choose to complete your order.
How do I remove items from my cart?
First, click on the “Cart” link in the upper-right corner of your computer screen. This will allow you to view all items currently in your cart. Once you have identified the item that you would like to delete, click on the “Trash” icon to the left of the item description and your changes will be immediately reflected.
How do I change the quantity of a particular item in my cart?
First, click on the “Cart” link in the upper-right corner of your browser window. This will allow you to view all items currently in your cart, as well as the quantities that you have chosen for each item. To change the quantity of an item in your cart, move your cursor to the box that appears under the "Quantity" header for this item and type in the quantity desired, then click the “Update cart” link. Once you do this, the quantity and associated dollar amount (if applicable) will automatically change and reflect the correct amounts for both the quantity and the cost.
How can I sign into my account on the Laura Janelle and edit the information in my account?
Currently, this feature is not available. Please call or e-mail our Customer Service department at customerservice@laurajanelle.com to update and/or edit your account.
How will I know that you have received my order?
After you complete the checkout process, a window will appear on your screen displaying your order number. When you close that modal window you then will be transferred to the "Orders" page where you can see your billing address, and the items that are being sent to your shipping address.

You will also receive confirmation via e-mail that we have received your order. (Please be sure to enter your e-mail address correctly on the order form so that we can be sure to deliver your confirmation to you.) Please keep this receipt for your reference.
What are your shipping and handling rates for online transactions?
All shipping is free when purchasing with a credit card. Please contact Customer Service if you require additional payment options to receive a shipping quote.
What if I need to change my address or suspend delivery?
Please contact our Customer Service department via phone or email with your changes as soon as possible. In your correspondence, please include your company name along with your old address, new address, as well as any special instructions you might have regarding suspending delivery of your order.
What are your payment options?
We accept all major credit cards (MasterCard, Visa, American Express, and Discover). If you require additional payment options, please contact our Customer Service department via phone or email for instructions.
What is your returns policy?
We want you to be completely satisfied with your order. If for some reason you are not and you wish to return your purchase, please contact our Customer Service department at 844-366-6900 to discuss your return.

If you have additional questions about our returns policy, please e-mail our Customer Service department at customerservice@laurajanelle.com.
What if I have received a defective item?
Please contact our Customer Service department within 30 days of the invoice date to report any defective merchandise and receive a prompt resolution.
How long after placing my order should I expect to receive my shipment?
All orders are shipped from our warehouse within 48 hours of the order being placed unless a later ship date has been requested. Purchases shipped anywhere in the continental United States will arrive within 7-10 business days of the order date. Orders being shipped outside of the United States generally arrive within 3-4 weeks of the order date.
Does your site install cookies?
Yes, we do install small packets of information on your computer temporarily. Cookies are used by us to streamline and enhance your ordering experience. In order to use our online store, you must have cookies enabled at all times.
Which browser should I use to view your site?
We highly recommend that you browse with the latest version of Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, or Apple Safari.
Can you ship your products anywhere?
Yes! Please contact our customer service department via phone or email if you are shipping outside of the United States to ensure all required shipping documents are completed.
